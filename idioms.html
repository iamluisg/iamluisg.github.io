ABOUT THE PROJECT

Office Idioms was an app developed several years ago while I was an ESL instructor in South Korea. It provides students wishing to further develop their understanding of the English language and its often used idioms. The app is aimed at professional students working within international companies and who often encounter idioms within the workplace. A new version with a major update to the UI is currently available on the app store!


This app was initially outsourced, but I completely wrote the newest version to accomodate all screen sizes and update the UI.
