ABOUT THE PROJECT

In talks with educators and through my own experience, a certain problem kept coming up in association with pronunciation; so the Oi Apps team put its heads together and identified a way in which we could utilize a smartphone to help solve the problem. Through a clever use of the self-facing camera and an instructional video, we have developed an app that will help anyone learning English to improve their pronunciation by allowing them to compare their mouth formations directly against a prerecorded instructor's. I am happy to announce that Inflect is now available on the app store!

The design of this app went through many iterations before finally settling on the current one. I was responsible for architecting the app, finding a graphic designer, coding the entire app, and editing the videos. I am very excited about how this app will help both educators and students acheive their goals.
