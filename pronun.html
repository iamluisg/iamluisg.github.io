ABOUT THE PROJECT

In talks with educators and through my own experience, a certain problem kept coming up in association with pronunciation, so I identified a way in which I could utilize a smartphone to help solve the problem. Through the use of the self-facing camera and an instructional video, I developed an app that will help anyone learning English to improve their pronunciation by allowing them to compare their mouth formations directly against a prerecorded instructor's.

Bloc code school even interviewed me and wrote about it in their blog. Take a look here.

The design of this app went through many iterations before finally settling on the current one. I developed the idea, worked out the UX, worked closely with a graphic designer to refine the UI, coded the app, and shot and edited the videos. I am very excited about how this app will help both educators and students acheive their goals.
